    Class Descriptions

Toddler Ballet (3-4 years old)
The goal of this class is to introduce your child to the world of dance in a positive and nurturing atmosphere.  Your tiny dancers will learn classical ballet steps, fun strengthening/stretching exercises,  travel like a butterfly and create a dance landscape with their imaginations.  Musicality, shapes and lines, directional awareness and class etiquette will also be a focus of the toddler program.  Musical accompaniment for this class varies from Tchaikovsky’s Nutcracker to Disney’s the Little Mermaid.

Ballet I (4-5 years old)
This class is a follow up to Toddler Ballet.  At this stage students are ready to incorporate more technical steps into their dance vocabulary.  In addition, they will focus on balance, musicality/rhythm, and body alignment.  Creativity continues to be cultivated as their own movement and ideas are incorporated into their class and performances.

Ballet II and up (5 years old and older)
Once they’ve developed a solid foundation, NVDS students are ready for a more structured ballet class.  At these levels, students will engage in a full barre, center work, and refine their technique and memory skills.  While these levels incorporate a more structured dance program, they also continue to cultivate expression thru movement and take into account each child’s comfort level and goals.

Adult Ballet
This class is offered to teenagers and adults. It is designed to work with the body's needs, building on alignment, strength and flexibility, as well as beauty and precision. The class follows a traditional format: barre work, stretching, center work, and across the floor concluding with an excerpt from a classical variation.

Hip Hop/Jazz (6 years +)
This class explores the fundamentals of Jazz technique including isolations, placement and leaps and turns set to age appropriate Hip Hop and Pop music.  students in this class will also explore the grounded nature of Hip Hop and learn new choreography set to their favorite music.
**Not currently available on our schedule, please contact NVDS if interested.

Observation Classes
All classes at NVDS are drop off and not open to viewing. Observation classes will be scheduled as part of the curriculum. There will be a number of days (disclosed on registration day) scheduled each semester when parents will be invited to attend their child's class.  This is to limit student distractions, so that they may get the most out of their hour.  Teachers will be available after class (and all classes) to discuss each child's progress, or parental concern.
