Our Policies

 

Tuition and Enrollment:

  • There is a non-refundable $30 registration fee due at enrollment and every Fall registration period thereafter.
  • Tuition is due, in full, upon enrollment on registration day or during the early registration periods.  NVDS has two enrollment periods.  One in the fall (August) and the second in the spring (January). 
  • We do not accept credit cards.  Please make all checks payable to Noe Valley Dance Space.  If making payments in cash, please have exact amount ready.

Absences and Tardiness:

  • Please notify NVDS if your child is going to be absent for any reason.
  • A maximum of 2 make-up classes are permitted per semester.
  • Classes must be made up within a two week window and should be schedule with the teacher or director.
  • Scheduled holidays are not eligible for make up classes.
  • Tardiness is frowned upon.  It can put the student at risk of injury by missing the warm up and is an unfair distraction to the class already in progress.  Please schedule a make up if you are going to be more than 10 minutes late to class.
  • Students arriving more than 5 minutes early to their class should be prepared to wait outside until the previous class has finished or the teacher has invited them in.  Please do not interrupt a class in session. If invited into a class which has not yet finished, only the student may enter and sit quietly until the class is dismissed.  If you prefer to wait with your child, please do so outside.  We have a drop off policy.

Class Observation:

  • All classes at NVDS are drop off and closed to viewing.  Please see our CLASSES page for more info.

Dress Code:

  • Girls:  All students should arrive to class in the appropriate class uniform. Class leotard, skirt (or belt), pink Ballet tights and pink Ballet slippers.  Our uniforms are mandatory and should be purchased through us upon enrollment.
  • Boys:  Please contact NVDS for dress options.
  • Shoes:  Girls are required to wear pink Ballet slippers to all classes.  Boys should may wear black or white.  Please make sure to label  the soles with your child’s name.  Shoes should not be worn outside, but reserved for the dance studio. 
  • Hair:  All hair should be worn up and away from the face.  Short hair should also be pulled back away from the face with a head band or clip.  Students in Ballet levels III and above should wear a bun to their Ballet class. 
  • Absolutely NO FOOD OR GUM in the dance studio.  Please keep snacks outside.
  • No jewelry or baggy clothing should be worn during class.  NVDS and dance stores alike carry Ballet wrap sweaters for purchase which may be worn during the colder months.  Baggy clothing and layering prohibits the teachers from accurately assessing alignment.
